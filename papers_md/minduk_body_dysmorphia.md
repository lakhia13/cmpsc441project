- Body dysmorphia

Body dysmorphia is when you have negative feelings about your body image. It's a mental health problem because you spend a lot of time worrying about your body image. And that affects your day-to-day life.

If you have these experiences, you might get a diagnosis of body dysmorphic disorder (BDD).

I would often pick up on tiny issues about my appearance and become obsessed with how I could change them

# NAMI Ending the Silence

**Source:** NAMI
**URL:** https://www.nami.org/Support-Education/NAMI-Ending-the-Silence

---

NAMI Ending the Silence is an engaging presentation that helps middle and high school aged youth learn about the warning signs of mental health conditions and what steps to take if you or a loved one are showing symptoms of a mental health condition.NAMI Ending the Silence is offered in-person by NAMI affiliates across the country and is alsonow available onlinewhen an in-person presentation is not available.

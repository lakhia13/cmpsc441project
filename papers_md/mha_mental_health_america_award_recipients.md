# Mental Health America award recipients

Each year, MHA recognizes exceptional advocates, peers, social workers, journalists, youth, and MHA affiliates with awards given during our Annual Conference. These awards honor individuals making a difference in the mental health field, particularly in prevention and early intervention.Learn more about our award categories.

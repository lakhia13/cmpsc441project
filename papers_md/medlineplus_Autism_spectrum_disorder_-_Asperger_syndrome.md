# Autism spectrum disorder - Asperger syndrome

Source: MedlinePlus
URL: https://medlineplus.gov/ency/article/001549.htm

Asperger syndrome is often considered a high functioning form of autism. It can lead to difficulty interacting socially, repeat behaviors, and clumsiness.

Asperger syndrome is a part of the larger developmental disorder category ofautism spectrum disorder.

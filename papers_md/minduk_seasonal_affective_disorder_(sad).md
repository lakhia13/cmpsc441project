- Seasonal affective disorder (SAD)

Seasonal affective disorder (SAD) is a type of depression. You can experience it during particular seasons or times of year.

There are things you can do to help you to cope. And there's treatment available for depression.

My energy levels fluctuated between autumn/winter and spring/summer. I absolutely dreaded the onset of darker days and felt that I was literally shutting down.

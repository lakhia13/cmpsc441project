- Suicidal thoughts and suicide prevention

Anyone can have suicidal thoughts. How long these thoughts last is different for everyone. It can be really hard.

Whether you're feeling low yourself or worried about someone else,we're here to support you. And to help you get through this.

Do not be afraid to reach out and say, “I am feeling rubbish. I am having thoughts of suicide. I need a little help.” Things can always get better.

## Support if you're feeling suicidal yourself

If you're about to attempt suicide, or have seriously harmed yourself – it's an emergency. You need urgent help. Find outhow to get help in a crisis.
